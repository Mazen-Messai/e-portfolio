About Me

After two rigorous years in Classe Préparatoire, where I extensively studied mathematics and computer science, I took France's prestigious engineering school entrance exams and successfully gained admission to ENSEEIHT, the second highest-ranked school in computer science. It has always been my dream school due to its renowned mathematics curriculum.

It was during Classe Préparatoire that I truly discovered my passion for problem-solving and computer science, a field that was once merely an interest. Since then, I have been certain that I want to pursue a career in research within computer science, particularly in areas that bridge the gap between mathematics and computing, such as machine learning.

However, my journey into problem solving began much earlier! At the age of ten, I qualified for the French Junior Mathematical Olympiad finals! Since that moment, I've found immense joy in solving complex problems, whether it be tackling LeetCode challenges or participating in competitions like SWERC or Prologin.

2048 AI

from 09/01/2023 to 05/01/2024

During my time in Classe Préparatoire, I wanted to dive deeper into game theory.
That's why I decided to study the 2048 game with a friend. Together, we developed several AIs and compared their performance. The objective was to build a solid methodology for creating AIs in games. We obtained some fascinating results and quickly realized that choosing an algorithm requires considering several factors, such as the role of randomness in the game, the influence of mathematical patterns, and more.
You can find an article about it on this website!
