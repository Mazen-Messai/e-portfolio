International mobility

Growing up, I had the unique opportunity to witness my father’s international career as a researcher. His travels exposed me to a world of science beyond borders, where knowledge is shared and expanded through collaboration. Watching him meet remarkable people with incredible expertise and exchange ideas across cultures transformed my early love for science into a true passion.

By following him on some of his trips, I had the privilege of meeting researchers from diverse countries, including Tunisia, Turkey, Spain, Vietnam, and many others. These encounters not only broadened my understanding of science but also showed me the joy and fulfillment of working in a global environment. It inspired me to view science not just as a subject, but as a way to build a career I will genuinely enjoy—one where I can contribute meaningfully to international projects and learn continuously from others around the world.
